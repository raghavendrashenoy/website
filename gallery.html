A Brief Story About The Company

At present the company has a team with experts who work to solve complex problems. They also have a strong network of contacts within the industry, enabling them to get access to the best suppliers and materials available.

We provide the following services

  • Mechanical design / Automotive design
  • Prototype development / New Product development
  • Press tool & mould manufacturing
  • Machining
  • Welding - MIG , TIG , LASER
  • Weld assemblies
  • Sheet metal and pipe' laser cutting & bending
  • Reverse engineering
